FAQ

How do I register for courses?

You can register for courses through the student portal. Visit the courses section and follow the instructions provided.

Here is some additional info about course registration, including deadlines and prerequisites.

Where can I find departmental updates?

Departmental updates are posted on the Mechatronics Department website under the "Updates" section.

Make sure to subscribe to notifications to stay updated on the latest announcements.

Who do I contact for academic support?

You can contact academic support by reaching out to the department's academic advisor via email or during office hours.

The academic advisor is available every Tuesday and Thursday from 10 AM to 2 PM for consultations.

What is the SWEP program?

The SWEP (Student Work Experience Program) is an opportunity for students to gain practical experience in their field of study.

Students are encouraged to apply early, and more details can be found on the SWEP portal on the university website.
